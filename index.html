Questions

Everything you want to know.

When does it launch?

We're building now. Waitlist members get early access before anyone else and will be notified the moment we go live.

Is it free?

The core app will be free. Some advanced features will have an optional subscription — early waitlist members get priority access to everything.

Is it only for tourists?

Not at all. Locals use it to discover their own island on weekends, plan staycations, and find spots they've never been to. PaDonde is for anyone who wants to explore Puerto Rico.

Does it work in Spanish?

Sí. PaDonde is fully bilingual — use it in English or Spanish, whichever feels natural to you.

How does the beach feature work?

Open any beach on the map and instantly see the hourly rain forecast, wave conditions (calm / moderate / rough), water temperature, and the best time window to go that day.

Can local businesses be listed?

Yes. If you own a restaurant, tour operation, or local spot in Puerto Rico, reach out — we'd love to feature you when we launch.
